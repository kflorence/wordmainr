Search Tips

  • Use a hyphen (dash) to give the location of a missing letter: w-rd or -are
  • Use an asterisk (star) for any number of unknown letters: lett* or *gry or ar*ct
  • Exclude words containing the letters that follow a caret (hat): ma-e ^kt
  • Or enter a few letters (without hyphens or asterisks) to see if they make any words.

    What is Wordmainr?

    Wordmainr is a free web service that helps you search for words and then in turn match them to domain listings, definitions, whois and registration information.

    Brought to you by kflorence.com (development), wordnik.com (definitions), domai.nr (domain listings and availabilities), and phpwhois (whois information).
